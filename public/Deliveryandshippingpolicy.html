Delivery and Shipping Policy

DELIVERY AND SHIPPING POLICY– 
What is Mydecorzone’s Shipping Policy?
 
We strive to deliver products purchased from mydocorzone in excellent condition and in the fastest time possible. As an introductory offer, all shipping is currently free on Dekorcompany.com. Shipping method for this will be standard and products shall take 7-8 business days to get delivered.

1.   If the order is cancelled before being shipped, lost or un-delivered to your preferred location, we will refund the complete order amount including any shipping charges, if paid online.

2.    If you return an order delivered to you, return-shipping charges will be deducted from your refund. For accounts whose return behaviour violates our fair usage policy, a delivery charge of Rs. 149 will be levied on all orders, irrespective of order value.
*Order value is calculated after applying discounts/VAT/GST or any other applicable charges.
 
 
FAIR USAGE POLICY –
 
We always strive hard to provide the best experience to our customers. However, we have noticed that few accounts abuse our liberal returns policy. These accounts typically return most of the items bought or choose to not accept our shipments. Hence, our regular customers are deprived of the opportunity to buy these items. To protect the rights of our customers, we reserve the right to collect shipping charge of Rs. 149 for all orders and disable cash on delivery option for accounts that have high percentage of returns and shipments not accepted by value of orders placed.
 
How do I check the status of my order?
 
Please log into your account and tap on “Order History” section under your Account page of Website/M-site. Click on the view order of the specific order you want to track. Then on the next page click on track order to check the exact order status.
 
 
How are orders placed on Mydecorzone delivered to me?
 
All orders placed on mydocorzone are dispatched through other courier partners such as Blue Dart, FedEx, Aramex Etc.
